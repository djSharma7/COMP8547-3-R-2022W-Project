BENEFITS FOR BUSINESS: prolongation of mine profitable activity, diversification of investment portfolio, reduction of economic industrial fluctuations risks, getting economic cluster effect; BENEFITS FOR REGION: effective operation of the city-forming enterprise, creation of new work places, increasing charges to the local budget, stimulation of innovation processes, forming of business environment.
